PARTICLES

 

213.  Adverbs, Prepositions, Conjunctions, and Interjections are called Particles. In their origin adverbs, prepositions, and conjunctions are either (1) case forms , actual or extinct, or (2) compounds and phrases. Particles cannot always be distinctly classified, for many adverbs are used also as prepositions and many as conjunctions (ยง 219 and ยง 222).
