CONJUGATIONS

4TH CONJUGATION

187. The 4th Conjugation includes all verbs which add ī- to the root to form the present stem.

Allen and Greenough’s New Latin Grammar for Schools and Colleges, edited by J.B. Greenough, G.L. Kittredge, A.A. Howard, and Benjamin L. D’Ooge. Boston: Ginn & Company, 1903.
